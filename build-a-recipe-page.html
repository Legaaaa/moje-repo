My Delicious Recipe

This is a simple and delicious recipe that everyone will love! Follow the instructions below to make it yourself.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, and eggs in a bowl.
  3. Add the vanilla extract and stir until smooth.
  4. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
